Friends Trip to Goa

Experience the vibrant culture and stunning beaches of Goa with your friends

How to Go

Travel to Goa by air or train to the Dabolim Airport or Madgaon Railway Station. Consider renting a vehicle or using local transport to explore the area.

Hostels to Stay

Zostel Goa

Zostel Goa is a popular hostel known for its vibrant atmosphere, social events, and comfortable accommodations.

The Funky Monkey Hostel

The Funky Monkey Hostel offers a fun and quirky environment, perfect for groups of friends looking to have a great time.

Red Door Hostel

Red Door Hostel provides a cozy and relaxed stay with a focus on creating a sense of community among travelers.

The Lost Hostel

The Lost Hostel offers comfortable accommodations and a laid-back atmosphere, making it an ideal place for friends to unwind.

Famous Places to Visit

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is known for its vibrant nightlife, water sports, and beach shacks, making it a perfect hangout spot for friends.

Fort Aguada

Fort Aguada offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea and provides a glimpse into Goa's colonial history.

Anjuna Flea Market

Anjuna Flea Market is a great place to shop for souvenirs, clothes, accessories, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere.

Palolem Beach

Palolem Beach offers a relaxed ambiance, beautiful sunsets, and opportunities for swimming and beach games.
