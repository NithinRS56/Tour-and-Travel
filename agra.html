Discover the historical charm and architectural wonders of Agra

History of Agra

Agra, located on the banks of the Yamuna River, has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It rose to prominence during the Mughal Empire and served as the capital city. Agra is renowned for its iconic monuments and cultural heritage.

Famous Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a stunning marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most iconic symbols of India.

Agra Fort

The Agra Fort is a UNESCO-listed fort complex that showcases a blend of Mughal and Islamic architectural styles. It offers breathtaking views of the Taj Mahal from its premises.

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri is a historical city built by Emperor Akbar, known for its well-preserved buildings, courtyards, and stunning architecture.
