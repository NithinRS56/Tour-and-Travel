History of Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is a bustling metropolis and the financial capital of India. It has a rich history, from its origins as a collection of islands inhabited by fishermen to becoming a major port and trade center during British colonial rule.

Famous Places to Visit

Gateway of India

The iconic Gateway of India is a historic monument and a popular gathering spot overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a picturesque promenade along the coastline, offering breathtaking views and a relaxing atmosphere.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

The Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning architecture and historical significance.

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves are ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures on Elephanta Island, a short ferry ride from Mumbai.
