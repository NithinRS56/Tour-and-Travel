History of Jaipur

Jaipur, often referred to as the Pink City, was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. The city is renowned for its well-planned layout, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. It has a rich history that reflects the grandeur of the Rajput era.

Famous Places to Visit

Amer Fort

The majestic Amer Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its artistic blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture.

Hawa Mahal

The iconic Hawa Mahal, or Palace of Winds, is a unique five-story structure with intricate latticework windows.

City Palace

The City Palace showcases a remarkable collection of artifacts, textiles, and art, providing a glimpse into Jaipur's royal history.

Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory featuring a collection of massive instruments built in the 18th century.
