Discover the industrial city and cultural heritage of Coimbatore

History of Coimbatore

Coimbatore, often called the "Manchester of South India," has a rich history as a prominent industrial and educational hub. The city's cultural heritage is intertwined with its growth as a major textile and manufacturing center.

Famous Places to Visit

Marudamalai Temple

Marudamalai Temple is a popular hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance.

Dhyanalinga Temple

Dhyanalinga Temple is a unique meditative space designed by Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev, offering a serene atmosphere for inner exploration.

Codissia Trade Fair Complex

Codissia Trade Fair Complex hosts various exhibitions, trade fairs, and events, contributing to Coimbatore's industrial and business activities.

Gedee Car Museum

Gedee Car Museum showcases a collection of vintage cars, showcasing the history and evolution of automobiles.
