History of Bangalore

Bangalore, now known as Bengaluru, is a bustling metropolis and the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka. It has a history dating back to ancient times, with influences from various dynasties and rulers. Today, Bengaluru is known for its technological advancements and diverse culture.

Famous Places to Visit

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh is a lush green garden with a stunning collection of plants and flowers, perfect for a leisurely stroll and enjoying nature.

Cubbon Park

Cubbon Park is a sprawling park in the heart of the city, offering a peaceful oasis with walking trails and architectural landmarks.

Bangalore Palace

The Bangalore Palace is a magnificent royal residence with Tudor-style architecture, housing beautiful interiors and artifacts.
