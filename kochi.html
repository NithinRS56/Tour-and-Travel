Explore Kochi (Cochin)

Discover the historic charm and cultural richness of Kochi

History of Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, has a rich history as a major port and trading hub in India. It has been influenced by various cultures, including Chinese, Portuguese, Dutch, and British. The city's heritage is reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and diverse communities.

Famous Places to Visit

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi is a historic neighborhood known for its colonial-era buildings, Chinese fishing nets, and vibrant arts scene.

Mattancherry Palace

Mattancherry Palace, also known as the Dutch Palace, features stunning murals and exhibits that showcase Kerala's history and culture.

Jewish Synagogue

The Jewish Synagogue in Kochi is one of the oldest in the country and is adorned with beautiful chandeliers and hand-painted tiles.

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a popular waterfront promenade where visitors can enjoy scenic views, shopping, and leisurely walks.
