History of Jodhpur

Jodhpur, often referred to as the "Blue City" due to its blue-painted buildings, is a historic city in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It was founded by Rao Jodha in the 15th century and has a captivating history of Marwar rulers, forts, and cultural heritage.

Famous Places to Visit

Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort is an imposing hilltop fortress with intricate architecture and stunning panoramic views of the city.

Jaswant Thada

Jaswant Thada is a marble mausoleum built in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II, featuring delicate carvings and serene gardens.

Umaid Bhawan Palace

Umaid Bhawan Palace is an opulent palace showcasing a blend of Rajput and Art Deco styles, now serving as a luxury hotel and museum.

Sardar Market

Sardar Market is a bustling marketplace known for its vibrant atmosphere, local handicrafts, spices, and textiles.
