Why Visit Rishikesh?

Rishikesh, often called the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a spiritual and adventure paradise. Explore serene landscapes, practice yoga, and immerse yourself in its rich culture.

Laxman Jhula

A famous suspension bridge over the Ganges River. It's a spiritual and architectural marvel.

Triveni Ghat

A sacred bathing spot at the confluence of three holy rivers. Witness the evening Ganga Aarti here.

Neelkanth Mahadev Temple

A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, situated amidst lush green mountains. A spiritual and scenic destination.

Shivpuri

Experience adventure activities like white-water rafting and camping in the picturesque setting of Shivpuri.

Beatles Ashram

Visit the ashram where The Beatles famously meditated. It's now a serene and artistic space.
