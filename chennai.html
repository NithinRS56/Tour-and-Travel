Discover the vibrant culture and heritage of Chennai

History of Chennai

Chennai has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It served as a major trading center under various dynasties and colonial powers. Today, it's a bustling metropolis known for its diverse culture, traditional arts, and modern developments.

Famous Places to Visit

Marina Beach

Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, offering a relaxing waterfront promenade and stunning sunsets.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Kapaleeshwarar Temple is a magnificent Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its intricate architecture and vibrant festivals.

Fort St. George

Fort St. George is a historic fortress built by the British East India Company, now housing a museum and government buildings.

Chennai Rail Museum

Chennai Rail Museum showcases India's railway heritage with a collection of vintage locomotives, carriages, and artifacts.
